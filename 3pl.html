What is 3PL Logistics?

Third-party logistics (3PL) involves outsourcing logistics services to a provider, such as warehousing, order fulfillment, transportation, and distribution. 3PL enables businesses to improve their supply chain efficiency, reduce costs, and focus on their core activities while leaving the logistics management to experts.
